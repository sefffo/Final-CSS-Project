Our Story

BookNest was started in 2020 by a group of teachers and parents who wanted to make reading fun for kids and teens. We believe every child deserves a great book!

We hand-pick every book in our store to make sure it's interesting, educational, and age-appropriate. Reading opens doors — and we want to open as many as possible.
